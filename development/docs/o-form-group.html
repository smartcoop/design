Form group

Use the form group object to control spacing and layout in forms.

Regular form grouping

#

The combination of a form label and an input field are usually wrapped inside a .o-form-group object. The consistent markup pattern makes it easy to manipulate grouped form elements.

<div class="o-form-group-layout o-form-group-layout--standard">
    <div class="o-form-group">
        <label class="o-form-group__label" for="nameId">Name</label>
        <div class="o-form-group__controls">
            <input class="c-input" type="email" value="John Doe" id="nameId" />
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="o-form-group">
        <label class="o-form-group__label" for="emailId">Email address</label>
        <div class="o-form-group__controls">
            <input class="c-input" type="email" value="user@name.com" id="emailId" />
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="o-form-group">
        <label class="o-form-group__label" for="passwordId">Password</label>
        <div class="o-form-group__controls">
            <input class="c-input" type="password" value="password" id="passwordId" />
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="o-form-group">
        <label class="o-form-group__label" for="selectId">Preference</label>
        <div class="c-select-holder">
            <select class="c-select" id="selectId">
                <option>Option A</option>
                <option>Option B</option>
                <option>Option C</option>
            </select>
        </div>
    </div>
    <div class="o-form-group">
        <input class="c-button c-button--primary" type="submit" value="Save" />
    </div>
</div>
