---
title: Scroll to
---

This interaction scroll to an specific element by clicking the initial one

To add the interaction, simply add <code>data-scroll-to data-scroll-target-id="id-of-the-target-element"</code> to the initial element. This will automatically scroll the page to the <code>#id-of-the-target-element</code> element. The scroll effect will be smooth.

Check out the real <a href="https://design.smart.coop/examples/template-examples/terms-and-conditions--acceptation.html">example here</a>
